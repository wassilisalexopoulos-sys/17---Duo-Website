Our Story is Brewed with Passion

Founded on the belief that exceptional coffee brings people together, DUO has been crafting memorable experiences for events across the region.

From Passion to Profession

DUO began as a simple idea: what if we could bring the artisanal coffee experience directly to people's most important moments? What started as a passion project between two friends who loved coffee has grown into a full-service event company that specializes in creating unforgettable coffee experiences.

Our journey started in a small garage where we experimented with different brewing methods, perfecting our craft one cup at a time. We quickly realized that coffee isn't just a beverage—it's a catalyst for connection, conversation, and celebration.

Today, we're proud to serve clients across the region, from intimate wedding ceremonies to large corporate conferences. Every event is an opportunity to share our passion for exceptional coffee and create moments that bring people together.
