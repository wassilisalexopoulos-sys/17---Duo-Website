Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for most events. For weddings and large corporate events, we suggest 6-8 weeks to ensure availability.

Do you provide equipment and supplies?

Yes! We bring all necessary equipment including professional espresso machines, grinders, and serving materials. We also provide eco-friendly cups and napkins.

What's included in your service?

Our service includes professional baristas, premium coffee beans, equipment setup and breakdown, custom branding options, and all necessary serving materials.

Do you offer catering options?

Absolutely! We provide a range of catering options from light pastries to full meal solutions. We can customize menus to complement your coffee service.

What are your minimum requirements?

We have a 2-hour minimum service time and can accommodate events with as few as 25 guests. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Do you travel outside the city?

Yes, we serve events throughout the region. Travel fees may apply for locations more than 50 miles from our base. Contact us for specific details.
