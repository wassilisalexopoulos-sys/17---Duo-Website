# 📸 How to Add Your Images to the DUO Website

## Quick Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Prepare Your Images
1. **Select your best coffee event photos** from the images you provided
2. **Convert them to black & white** or desaturated for the minimalist aesthetic
3. **Resize them** to the recommended dimensions (see below)

### Step 2: Save Images with Correct Names
Save your images in the `images/` folder with these exact filenames:

#### Homepage Hero Image
- **Filename**: `hero-barista-pouring.jpg`
- **Description**: Barista pouring steamed milk into coffee cup
- **Size**: 800x600px minimum

#### About Page Team Photo
- **Filename**: `team-photo.jpg`
- **Description**: Your two team members behind the coffee counter
- **Size**: 600x400px minimum

#### Gallery Images (choose your best 9 photos)
- `mobile-coffee-bar.jpg` - Mobile coffee bar setup
- `barista-preparation.jpg` - Barista preparing coffee
- `layered-drinks.jpg` - Beautiful layered specialty drinks
- `coffee-setup.jpg` - Professional coffee equipment
- `team-at-work.jpg` - Team working at event
- `iced-coffee-service.jpg` - Iced coffee service with menu
- `coffee-pouring.jpg` - Expert coffee pouring technique
- `event-customers.jpg` - Happy customers at event
- `matcha-preparation.jpg` - Specialty matcha preparation

#### Services Page Images
- Use the same images as gallery, just save them with the service-specific names

### Step 3: Image Optimization
1. **File size**: Keep under 500KB each
2. **Format**: Use JPEG for photos
3. **Quality**: High enough to look professional, but compressed for web

### Step 4: Test Your Website
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Check that all images load properly
3. Test on mobile devices

## 🎨 Image Style Guidelines

### Black & White Aesthetic
- Convert color images to grayscale
- Increase contrast slightly for better impact
- Ensure DUO branding is visible
- Maintain professional quality

### Composition Tips
- Focus on coffee preparation and service
- Show professional equipment and setup
- Include team members when appropriate
- Capture the quality and attention to detail

## 🚀 Once Images Are Added

Your website will automatically display the images instead of the placeholders. The website is fully functional and ready to use!

## Need Help?

If you need assistance with image editing or have questions about the setup, the website will work perfectly with the placeholder images until you're ready to add your photos.
